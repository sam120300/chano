Case Study

SCRAPDX: approval workflow with loss-tracking analytics.

ProjectSCRAPDX FocusScrap material processing OutputOnline approval and analytics dashboard EnvironmentIn-house company system

Overview

SCRAPDX was designed to process scrap material records through an online approval workflow while giving the company a clearer dashboard for tracking and reporting losses.

The project reflects the kind of application work I enjoy most: replacing slow manual coordination with structured data, status visibility, and repeatable reporting.

Problem

Manual visibility gaps

Scrap material processing needed clearer tracking, cleaner approval movement, and faster report preparation.

Solution

Digital workflow

The system organizes requests, approval states, and related records so teams can follow the process online.

Impact

Analytics-ready data

Dashboard reporting helps the company monitor losses and prepare clearer summaries from system data.

More Systems

Same pattern, different departments.

My other projects follow the same direction: HR document requests, labor accrual reports, accounting forms, environmental checksheets, work-hour compliance, and archive systems. The goal is always to make business processes easier to submit, approve, monitor, and report.
